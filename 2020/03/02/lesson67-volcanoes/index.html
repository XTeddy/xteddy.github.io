Lesson67 Volcanoes

发表于 2020-03-02 更新于 2020-03-03 分类于 英语 ， 新概念英语二 Valine：

复习can/be able to「43课」、manage to。

课文

Volcanoes「火山」


Haroun Tazieff, the Polish scientist, has spent his lifetime studying active volcanoes and deep caves in all parts of the world. In 1948, he went to Lake Kivu in the Congo to observe a new volcano which he later named Kituro. Tazieff was able to set up his camp very close to the volcano while it was erupting violently. Though he managed to take a number of brilliant photographs, he could not stay near the volcano for very long. He noticed that a river of liquid rock was coming towards him. It threatened to surround him completely, but Tazieff managed to escape just in time. He waited until the volcano became quiet and he was able to return two days later. This time, he managed to climb into the mouth of Kituro so that he could take photographs and measure temperatures. Tazieff has often risked his life in this way. He has been able to tell us more about active volcanoes than any man alive.

译：
波兰科学家哈罗恩·塔捷耶夫花了毕生的精力来研究世界各地的活火山和深洞. 1948年他去了刚果的基伍湖, 对一座后来被他命名为基图罗的新火山进行观察. 当火山正在猛烈地喷发时, 塔捷耶夫有办法把帐篷搭在离它非常近的地方. 尽管他设法拍了一些十分精彩的照片, 但他却不能在火山附近停留太长的时间. 他发现有一股岩浆正向他流过来, 眼看就要将他团团围住, 但塔捷耶夫还是设法及时逃离了. 他等到火山平静下来, 两天以后又返回去. 这次他设法爬进了基图罗火山口, 以便能拍摄照片和测试温度. 塔捷耶夫经常冒这样的生命危险. 他能告诉我们的有关活火山的情况比任何在世的人都要多.

词

liquid：adj.液态的
solid「adj.固体的」
gaseous「adj.气态的」

violently：adv.猛烈地，剧烈地
The building shook violently in the earthquake
violent「adj.猛烈的，剧烈的」
violence「n.暴力」

句子

表示过去成功/没有成功做到某事

过去成功做到
was/were able to do sth.
managed to do sth.
succeed in doing sth.
过去没有成功做到
上面三种加否定
could not do sth.
failed to do sth.

课文

1.Tazieff was able to set up his camp very close to the volcano while it was erupting violently.
close「靠近(63课)」
区别can/be able to(43课)
相同点：都表示能力，能够
（can/could;be able to时态更灵活）
不同点：
表示过去已经成功做到，用was/were able to，不用could「不一定能做」
can可以表示猜测，允许

2.Though he managed to take a number of brilliant photographs, he could not stay near the volcano for very long.
managed to do sth.「设法去做…（一定能做到）」 = was/were able to do sth.

错题

选择

1.He escaped just in time. He ____ escaped.
A. just only
B. only
C. soon
D. only just
选D

2.He waited until the volcano became quiet. He waited ____ quiet.
A. to the volcano to become
B. the volcano to become
C. the volcano became
D. for the volcano to become
waited不及物，后只能跟for引导的短语，选D

3.He has been able to tell us a lot ____ volcanoes.
A. considering
B. on the subject of
C. in relation
D. referring
A「考虑到」,B「关于，论及」,C「应为in relation to（关于）」,D「应是referring to（论及，谈论）」，选B

4.Tazieff is a Polish scientist. He comes from ____.
A. Pole
B. Poland
C. Polish
D. the Pole
A「波兰人」,B「波兰」,C「波兰的，波兰语」,D「波兰人」，选B

5.He has told us more than any ____ person.
A. alive
B. lively
C. live
D. living
选一个名词前定语，A「活着的，常作表语，作定语时放名词后」,B「快活的，活泼的」,C「活着的，有生命的」,D「活着的」，a live person「精力充沛」，a living person「活着」，选D
