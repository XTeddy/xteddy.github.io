Lesson63 She was not amused

发表于 2020-02-24 分类于 英语 ， 新概念英语二 Valine：

复习宾语从句、间接引语「15、39课」。

课文

She was not amused「她并不觉得好笑」


Jeremy Hampden has a large circle of friends and is very popular at parties. Everybody admires him for his great sense of humour – everybody, that is, except his six-year-old daughter, Jenny. Recently, one of Jeremy’s closest friends asked him to make a speech at a wedding reception. This is the sort of thing that Jeremy loves. He prepared the speech carefully and went to the wedding with Jenny. he had included a large number of funny stories in the speech and, of course, it was a great success. As soon as he had finished, Jenny told him she wanted to go home. Jeremy was a little disappointed by this but he did as his daughter asked. On the way home, he asked Jenny if she had enjoyed the speech. To his surprise, she said she hadn’t. Jeremy asked her why this was so and she told him that she did not like to see so many people laughing at him!

译：
杰里米.汉普登交际甚广，是各种聚会上深受大家欢迎的人。人人都钦佩他那绝妙的幽默感 – 人人，就是说，除他6岁的女儿珍妮之外的每一个人。最近，杰里米的一个最亲密的朋友请他在一个婚礼上祝词。这正是杰里米喜欢做的事情。他认真准备了讲稿，带着珍妮一道去参加了婚礼。他的祝词里面加进了大量逗人的故事，自然大获成功。他刚一讲完，珍妮就对他说她要回家。这不免使杰里米有点扫兴，但他还是按照女儿的要求做了。在回家的路上，他问珍妮是否喜欢他的祝词。使他吃惊的是，她说她不喜欢。杰里米问他为何不喜欢，她说她不愿意看到那么多的人嘲笑他!

词

circle：n.圈子
draw a circle「画一个圈（具体）」
a large circle of friends「一大圈朋友，交际广（抽象）」

admire：v.赞扬
admire sb. for sth.「因为…赞扬某人」
We admire him for his knowledge「我们因为他只是渊博而赞扬他」

close：读/s/，adj.亲密的，近的（距离）
Our home is close to the school.「我们的家离学校近」
get close to…「接近…」
closest friends「最亲密的朋友们」
读/z/，v.关闭

sort：n.种类（同type，kind）
this sort/kind/type of thing「这种事」
sort/kind of「稍微，有点」

laugh：v.大笑，嘲笑（介词用at）
Everybody laughed out loud when he said that.「他说那话时大家都放声大笑」
She did not like to see so many people laughing at him.「她不愿看到那么多人嘲笑他」
laugh at后跟物，意为「因…而发笑」：
You’re very kind to laugh at my funny stories.「我很感激你因我讲的逗人的故事而发笑」

句子

开头语

You must be very annoyed with me for…「你一定很生我…..的气」
I have just heard that…「我刚听说…」*

宾语从句的位置

1.动宾「vt.（及物动词） + 从句」
I know that you are right.「我知道你是对的（单宾）」
He tells me that you are right.「他告诉我你是对的（双宾）」

2.介宾「prep. + 从句」
He was astonished at what he found.「他惊讶于他发现了什么」

3.形容词 + 宾语从句
they were also quite sure that a cigarette end did not start the fire「他们很确定不是烟头引起的火灾」

课文

1.Recently, one of Jeremy’s closest friends asked him to make a speech at a wedding reception.
one of 后面加可数名词的复数「…的之一」
ask sb. to do sth.「要求某人做某事」
make a speech「作演讲」

2.This is the sort of thing that Jeremy loves.
n + that从句：定语从句或同位语从句
We have no idea that the boss come.「老板来了我们还没有主意（同位语从句）」
It is the most interesting movie that I have ever seen.「这是我曾经以来看过最有趣的电影」

3.he had included a large number of funny stories in the speech
include「v.包括，包含（抽象意义上的）」
contain「v.包括，包含（能装进去的，见container「n.容器」）」
a number of…「大量」，跟可数名词复数
a large/great number of…「大量」，跟可数名词复数

4.but he did as his daughter asked.
as + 句子「按照…方式」
引导方式状语从句
When in Rome, do as Romans do.「在罗马，按照罗马人的方式做（入乡随俗）」
Do it as I told you last week.「按照上周我给你说的做」

错题

回答问题

1.Is Jeremy Hampden greatly admired for his great sense of homour or not? What was he invited to do? Did he immediately agree to do so or not?(When Jeremy…who is…)
When Jeremy Hampden, who is greatly admired for his great sense of humour, was invited to make a speech at wedding reception, he immediately agreed to do so.

选择

1.Everybody admires him because ____ a great sense of humour.
A. he’s got
B. he is
C. he got
D. has he
he’s = he has，选A

2.____ Jenny, everyone admires him for his great sense of humour.
A. Except for
B. Apart
C. Except
D. Unless
A「除…以外」,B「adv.分开，离开」,C「除…外，不能用于句首」,D「除非，如果不」，选A

3.It was a great success. He succeeded ____ everyone laugh.
A. to make
B. for making
C. in make
D. in making
succeeded后面只能跟介词in加动名词表示「成功做某事」，选D

4.Jeremy was little disappointed. He was ____ disappointed.
A.little
B.somehow
C.enough
D.somewhat
A「有点儿，有些」,B「不知怎么地，以某种方式」,C「足够」,D「一点，几分」，选D
