Lesson26 The best art critics

发表于 2019-10-02 分类于 英语 ， 新概念英语二 Valine：

新概念英语第二册第26课课文笔记。

恭喜bdongs，您升为26级小白，认真阅读完即可升为27级小白。

课文

The best art critics「最佳美术评论家」


I am an art student and I paint a lot of pictures. Many people pretend that they understand modern art. They always tell you what a picture is ‘about’. Of course, many pictures are not ‘about’ anything. They are just pretty patterns. We like them in the same way that we like pretty curtain material. I think that young children often appreciate modern pictures better than anyone else. They notice more. My sister is only seven, but she always tells me whether my pictures are good or not. She came into my room yesterday. ‘ What are you doing ?’ she asked. ‘ I’m hanging this picture on the wall,’ I answered. ‘It’s a new one. Do you like it ?’ She looked at it critically for a moment.’ It’s all right,’ she said, ‘but isn’t it upside down ?’ I looked at it again. She was right! It was!

译：
我是个学艺术的学生，画了很多画。有很多人装成很懂现代艺术的样子，总是告诉你一幅画的“意思”是什么。当然，有很多画是什么“意思”也没有的。它们就是些好看的图案，我们喜爱它们就像我们喜欢漂亮的窗帘布一样。我觉得小孩子们往往比任何人都更能欣赏现代绘画，他们观察到的东西更多。我的妹妹只有7岁，但她总能说出我的画是好还是坏。昨天她到我房里来了。

词

paint：
v.画，在…上涂油漆，涂抹
paint pictures「画画」
paint a house「给房子刷漆」
n.涂料，油漆，化妆品
painting「n.油画，水彩画，绘画艺术 」

hang：
v.悬挂，吊「hung, hung」
hang this picture on the wall
绞死，上吊「hanged, hanged」
We must all hang together, or assuredly we shall all hang separately.「我们必须团结一致，否则会被一一绞死」
垂下
John was very tired. He sat in a chair and hung(down) his head.「约翰非常疲倦，他垂着头坐在椅子上」
安装…使能转动/摆动
Have you hung the door?「你把门装上了吗？」

notice：v. 注意到，察觉到
Did you notice him leaving?「他离开的时候你发现了吗？」
n.注意，察觉
The girl in red caught his notice.「那位穿红衣服的姑娘引起了他的注意」
n.通知
I know there’s meeting, because someone put up a notice outside the Town Hall.「我知道有个集会，因为有人在市政厅外面贴了通知」

句子

宾语从句

定义：一个句子作宾语放到另一个句子中「连接词+陈述句」

写法
陈述句变宾语从句
「They understand modern art」
Many people pretend (that) they understand modern art.
特殊疑问句变宾语从句：调整语序「主语提前」
「What is a picture ‘about’?」
They always tell you what a picture is ‘about’.
Can you tell me when we will set out?「你能告诉我什么时候我们将会出发吗？」
一般疑问句变宾语从句：调整语序「主语提前」 + 前加whether/if「尽量都用whether」
Are my pictures good or not?
She always tells me whether/if my pictures are good or not.

位置
动宾结构「动单宾（及物动词后跟一个宾语）和动双宾（及物动词后跟双宾语）」
Many people pretend that they understand modern art.「动单宾」
They always tell you what a picture is ‘about’.「动双宾」
介宾结构
He was astonished at what he found.

课文句子

We like them in the same way that we like pretty curtain material.「我们喜爱它们就像我们喜欢漂亮的窗帘布一样」
in the same way that/as是连接短语，用于比较，译为「像/和…一样」
She walks in the same way that/as her sister did.「她走路的姿势和她姐姐以前走路姿势一样」
