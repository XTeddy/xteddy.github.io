Lesson27 A wet night

发表于 2019-10-04 更新于 2020-03-06 分类于 英语 ， 新概念英语二 Valine：

新概念英语第二册第27课课文笔记。

课文

A wet night「雨夜」


Late in the afternoon, the boys put up their tent in the middle of a field. As soon as this was done, they cooked a meal over an open fire. They were all hungry and the food smelled good. After a wonderful meal, they told stories and sang songs by the campfire. But some time later it began to rain. The boys felt tired so they pet out the fire and crept into their tent. Their sleeping bags were warm and comfortable, so they all slept soundly. In the middle of the night, two boys woke up and began shouting. The tent was full of water! They all leapt out of their sleeping bags and hurried outside. It was raining heavily and they found that a stream had formed in the field. The stream wound its way across the field and then flowed right under their tent!

译：
傍晚时分，孩子们在田野中央搭起了帐篷。这件事刚刚做完，他们就在篝火上烧起了饭。他们全都饿了，饭菜散发出阵阵香味。他们美美地吃了一顿饭后，就围在营火旁讲起了故事，唱起了歌。但过了一阵子。天下起雨来，于是他们扑灭了篝火，钻进了帐篷。睡袋既暖和又舒服，所以，他们都睡得很香。午夜前后，有两个孩子醒了，大声叫了起来。原来帐篷里到处都是水！他们全都跳出睡袋，跑到外面。雨下得很大，他们发现地上已经形成了一条小溪。那小溪弯弯曲曲穿过田野，然后正好从他们的帐篷底下流过去。

词

smell：
v.闻，闻起来
The food smelled good「食物闻起来是好的（smell作系动词，后跟形容词）」
She smelled the meat to see if it was fresh.「她闻这肉看看是否是新鲜的（smell后跟名词）」
n.气味
I can’t stand the smell in this room.「我不能忍受这房间里的气味」

wonderful：adj.极好的
同义词
fabulous/excellent/fantastic/terrific

creep：v.爬行「crept/crept」
同样的变形
sleep slept slept
keep kept kept
sweep swept swept

sleeping bag：睡袋
doing「表示功能、用途」
a swimming pool「游泳池」
a fishing boat「渔船」
a packing case「货运箱」
a waiting room「等候室」

soundly：adv.香甜的
sleep soundly/deeply/well「睡得好」
be fast asleep「睡得好」

wind：v.蜿蜒「wound/wound」
wind one’s way「蜿蜒出一条路来」
The stream wound its way across the field.「小溪在田野蜿蜒出一条路来」
wind round sth.「蜿蜒绕着某事」
A snake wound round the wires.「一条蛇缠绕在电线上」

form：
v.形成
During the conversation, an idea formed in his mind.「在谈话的过程中，他有了一个主意」
n.形状，外形
I don’t like the form of the cake.「我不喜欢这个蛋糕的形状」
n.表格
If you want to enter of the competition, you must fill in these forms.「如果你想报名参加比赛，你必须填写这些表格」

句子

课文句子

Late in the afternoon, the boys put up their tent in the middle of a field.「傍晚时分，孩子们在田野中央搭起了帐篷」
late in the afternoon「傍晚」
early in the morning「一大早」
in the middle of…「在…中间，后可接地点、时间等」
in the middle of a field
in the middle of the night
in centre of…「在…中间，后只接地点」
in the centre of Australia

But some time later it began to rain.「但过了一阵子。天下起雨来」
some time「一段时间，时间段」
sometime「有时，时间点」

two boys woke up and began shouting.「有两个孩子醒了，大声叫了起来」
wake woke woken
wake waked waked
begin doing sth. = begin to do sth.「开始做某事」
began to shout
